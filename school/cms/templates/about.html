Welcome to PM SHRI Government Senior Secondary School Nagpur

At PM SHRI Government Senior Secondary School Nagpur, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and enriching learning experience for students from 6th to 12th grades. With a focus on academic excellence, vocational education, and holistic development, we strive to empower our students to excel academically, pursue their passions, and succeed in their chosen career paths.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire a love for learning, foster critical thinking skills, and cultivate values of integrity, respect, and responsibility in our students. We are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive school community where every student feels valued, empowered, and prepared for success in college, career, and beyond.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We maintain high academic standards and offer a diverse range of educational programs that cater to students' interests, talents, and career aspirations.
  • Integrity: We uphold principles of honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct, fostering a culture of trust, integrity, and personal responsibility.
  • Respect: We celebrate diversity, promote empathy, and foster mutual respect among students, teachers, staff, and the broader community.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork to achieve common goals, solve complex problems, and make a positive impact on society.
  • Personal Growth: We support the holistic development of our students by providing opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and community engagement.

Academic Programs

Vocational Education

  • Automobile Vocational Program: Our automobile vocational program offers hands-on training and theoretical knowledge in automotive technology, maintenance, and repair, preparing students for careers in the automotive industry.
  • Beauty and Wellness Vocational Program: Our beauty and wellness vocational program provide students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge in cosmetology, skincare, and wellness practices, preparing them for careers in the beauty and wellness industry.

Streams for 11th and 12th Grades

  • Arts Stream: Our arts stream offers a diverse range of subjects, including literature, history, geography, fine arts, and performing arts, allowing students to explore their creativity and express themselves through various mediums.
  • Commerce Stream: Our commerce stream focuses on subjects such as accounting, economics, business studies, and entrepreneurship, preparing students for careers in finance, commerce, and business management.
  • Science Stream: Our science stream emphasizes subjects such as physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and computer science, providing students with a strong foundation in scientific principles and preparing them for careers in science, engineering, medicine, and technology.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academic programs, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, clubs, and sports teams to enhance students' educational experience, promote leadership skills, and foster a sense of community.

Our Faculty

Our dedicated team of educators are passionate professionals who are committed to providing students with the knowledge, skills, and support they need to succeed. With expertise in their respective fields and a passion for teaching, our teachers inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning in their students.
