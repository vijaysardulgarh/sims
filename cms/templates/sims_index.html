About SIMS

Overview

SIMS is a robust web-based application designed to streamline various administrative and academic processes within educational institutions. From student enrollment and attendance tracking to grade management and reporting, SIMS offers a wide range of features to meet the needs of educators, administrators, students, and parents alike.

Features

  • Student Management
  • Staff Management
  • Events Management
  • Timetable Management
  • Library Management
  • Noticeboard/Announcements
  • Parent-Teacher Communication
  • Fee Management
  • Mobile Accessibility
