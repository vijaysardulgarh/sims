Admission Procedure

We welcome you to be a part of our school family. The admission process is simple and transparent.

Step 1: Application to the Principal

Parents/Guardians should submit a written application addressed to the Principal requesting admission of their child. The application should include:

  • Child’s Name
  • Date of Birth & Class seeking admission
  • Parent’s Name & Contact Details
  • Reason for seeking admission (transfer/new admission)

Sample Format

To  
The Principal  
[School Name]  
[School Address]  

Subject: Application for Admission  

Respected Sir/Madam,  

I, [Parent’s Name], parent of [Child’s Name], wish to seek admission for my ward in your esteemed institution in Class [class name]. My child’s date of birth is [DD/MM/YYYY].  

We request you to kindly consider our application and grant admission. We assure you of our full cooperation in upholding the school’s values and discipline.  

Thanking you,  

Yours sincerely,  
[Parent’s Name]  
Contact No: [XXXXXXXXXX]

Step 2: Registration

Collect the Admission Form from the Class Incharge or download it from the school website. Submit the completed form along with the required documents (see list below) to the Class Incharge.

Documents Required

For all applicants

  • Birth Certificate of the child
  • Child’s Aadhaar Card (or valid Government ID if Aadhaar not available)
  • Passport-size photographs of the child and both parents

If applicable

  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (for admission to Class II onwards)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Parent ID proofs

  • Father’s Aadhaar Card
  • Mother’s Aadhaar Card

Education IDs (as available)

  • Permanent Education Number (PEN)
  • APAAR ID (One Nation One Student ID)
Note: Please bring self-attested photocopies of all documents along with originals for verification.

Admission Timeline

  • Admissions open from 1st April to 30th April every year.
  • Late admissions may be considered subject to seat availability and government orders.

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 3+ years as on 31st March of the admission year
  • Class I: 6+ years as on 31st March of the admission year
  • Higher classes: As per age-appropriate eligibility

Fee Structure

  • No admission fee charged for classes Nursery to VIII as per RTE norms.
  • For higher classes, applicable fee details can be obtained from the school office.
